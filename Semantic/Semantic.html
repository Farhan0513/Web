    • Football

    Football is a world famous game. It is played between two teams consisting of eleven players. Football was invented in China. Football is played on a flat grass field. The field has two bars to score goals. Three referees control the game, one being the main referee and the other two being offside referees. The length of a football game is ninety minutes. Some world famous teams have made football famous; like FC Barcelona, Bayern Munich, AC Milan, Manchester United and Real Madrid[cheaters].

    Dr. Muhammad Yunus

    After the quota movement, former prime minister Sheikh Hasina had resigned in the 5th August. Bangladesh needed a worthy prime minister at that time. So, they requested nobel price winner Dr. Muhammad Yunus to be the new prime minister of Bangladesh. Dr. Muhammad Yunus is famous all over the world. He was born in the Chattogram district of Bangladesh. He won a nobel price on 'peace' in 2006.

    • Who We Are

    Welcome to The Flowers K.G. and High School, a place where education meets inspiration. Established in the 5th May of 1979, our institution has been dedicated to empowering students with knowledge, skills, and values that prepare them to thrive in a dynamic and ever-changing world.

    Our History

    The Flowers K.G. and High School began its journey in the 5th May of 1979, in the Court Road of the Moulvibazar district, with a vision to create an inclusive learning environment that nurtures young minds. Starting with just some students and a very litte amount of teachers in a modest setting, the school has grown into a leading educational institution over the years. Guided by our founders' dedication to excellence, we have continuously evolved to incorporate modern teaching methods, advanced facilities, and a strong focus on holistic development. Throughout our history, we have celebrated countless milestones, including the achievements of our students, recognition for our academic programs, and the unwavering support of our community.

    Our Vision

    To foster a community of lifelong learners who are compassionate, innovative, and equipped to make meaningful contributions to society. There are 49 teachers to provide proper guidelines to the students. Additionally, there are 19 staffs who are always responsible.

    Our Mission

    Provide a holistic education that combines academic excellence with character development. Integrate modern technology and innovative teaching practices to enhance learning. Encourage creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. Promote inclusivity and respect for diversity.

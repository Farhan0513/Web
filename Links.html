The Weeknd

The Weeknd is a Canadian singer. His real name is Abel Tesfaye. Although he is Canadian, he has Ethiopian origins. He has written a lot of songs. His famous songs are:

  • Starboy
  • Blinding Lights
  • Reminder
  • The Hills
  • Can't Feel My Face
  • Heartless

His most famous albums are:

  1. After Hours
  2. Starboy
  3. Beauty Behind The Madness
  4. My Dear Melancholy,
  5. House Of Balloons
